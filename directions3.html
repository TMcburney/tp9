• Place large skillet over medium-high heat, then add olive oil, bell pepper and cook for 6 min while stirring occationally.
  • Add the ground beef and break apart with a wooden spoon and cook for 6-8 min, stirring occationally until beef is cooked through.
  • Add tomatosauce, brown sugar, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, and stir to combine.
  • Reduce heat so liquid is smirring (low to medium-low) for 10-12 minutes until sauce thickens.
  • Remove from heat and let the meat rest for 5 min before serving.
